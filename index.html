Delicious Raspberry Bar Recipe!

Raspberry season is here. Washington state has been super hot, and fruit that we don't normally see until the end of July early August is already in the store. I've been dropping by Pinterest every couple of days to keep an eye out for new recipes. I searched on raspberries and came up with this link: http://www.crumblycookie.net/2008/03/02/raspberry-bars/

This recipe is pretty darn tasty. I've already made it two days in a row for two different parties. I recommend doubling the amount of jam, frui, and lemon juice.
